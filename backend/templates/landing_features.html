Powerful Features for Every Team

Comprehensive tools designed to increase productivity, transparency, and accountability in your organization. Track, measure, and optimize workforce performance with enterprise-grade reliability.

App & Website Tracking

Real-time monitoring of active applications and websites. Get detailed insights into what employees are working on throughout the day with automatic time tracking.

Smart Screenshots

Automatic screenshot capture at configurable intervals. Secure storage with privacy controls. Visual confirmation of work activities and task completion.

Idle Time Detection

Automatic detection and logging of inactive periods. Smart alerts for idle time. Helps identify productivity gaps and optimize work schedules.

Advanced Dashboards

Customizable real-time dashboards for admins and employees. Visual analytics showing productivity trends, time usage, and team performance metrics.

Task Management

Built-in task assignment and tracking. Monitor task progress, deadlines, and completion rates. Integrate tasks with time tracking for complete visibility.

Detailed Reports

Generate comprehensive reports on productivity, time usage, and KPIs. Daily, weekly, and monthly analytics. Export in multiple formats for presentations.

Activity Logging & Monitoring

Maintain detailed logs of all employee activities throughout their work sessions. Our intelligent system tracks every action for complete transparency and accountability.

  • Real-time Activity Logging: Every action timestamped and stored
  • Application Usage: Detailed breakdown of time spent per application
  • Website Browsing: Track visited websites and time spent
  • Session Management: Automatic start/stop tracking for work sessions
  • Activity Timeline: Complete audit trail of daily activities

Analytics & Insights

Transform raw data into actionable insights. Our analytics engine provides comprehensive views of team productivity, work patterns, and performance trends.

  • Productivity Metrics: Track productivity scores and trends
  • Time Distribution: Analyze how time is spent across tasks
  • Team Benchmarks: Compare performance across team members
  • Trend Analysis: Identify patterns and improvements over time
  • Custom Reports: Create tailored reports for specific metrics

Security & Privacy Controls

Enterprise-grade security with granular privacy controls. Ensure compliance with data protection regulations while maintaining organizational security.

  • Data Encryption: End-to-end encryption of all data
  • Access Controls: Role-based permissions and access levels
  • Privacy Settings: Employees control their own privacy preferences
  • Audit Logs: Complete audit trail of all system access
  • GDPR Compliance: Built-in compliance with data protection laws
